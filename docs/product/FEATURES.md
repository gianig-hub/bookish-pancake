# EK Marketplace — Feature Overview

## Core Features

### 1. Services Marketplace

Browse and post HVAC and refrigeration service listings.

- Installation services (AC, refrigeration, cold rooms)
- Maintenance and servicing
- Emergency repair services
- Commissioning and decommissioning
- PAT testing and compliance

### 2. Equipment Marketplace

Buy and sell equipment, parts, and tools.

- Air conditioning units (split, multi-split, HVAC systems)
- Commercial refrigeration units
- Cold room panels and components
- Refrigeration compressors and parts
- Tools and gauges

### 3. Businesses / Dealers Directory

Verified business profiles for installers, shops, and suppliers.

- Business profile with services offered
- Location and service area
- Contact details and website
- Customer reviews (Phase 3)
- Certifications and accreditations

### 4. Wanted Ads

Post requirements and receive quotes from sellers/installers.

- Equipment wanted posts
- Service requirement posts
- Project tender notices
- Expiry and renewal system

### 5. AI-Assisted Features

- **Smart search** — natural language to structured filters
- **Listing assistant** — AI-generated title and description
- **Category suggestions** — auto-categorise from description
- **Moderation scoring** — spam and scam detection
- **Support chatbot** — FAQ-driven help assistant

### 6. Guides / Knowledge Hub

- Installation guides and how-tos
- Buying guides (what to look for)
- Regulatory compliance guides (F-Gas, etc.)
- Industry terminology glossary
- FAQ for buyers and sellers
