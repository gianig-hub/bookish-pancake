# EK Marketplace — Project Summary

## Overview
EK Marketplace is an **AI-first UK vertical marketplace** focused on:

- Air conditioning
- Refrigeration
- Cold rooms
- Freezer rooms
- Related equipment, parts, tools, and services

The project is designed as a **specialist marketplace/classifieds platform**, not just a business website.

It combines:

- **Services Marketplace**
- **Equipment Marketplace**
- **Businesses / Dealers Directory**
- **Wanted Ads**
- **Guides / Blog**
- **AI-assisted search, posting, moderation, and support**

> Working project/domain name is still being updated. Existing “Everything Kold” references are legacy project context and will be replaced by the final brand/domain.

---

## Main Vision
Build a **UK-first SEO-led marketplace** where users can:

- find AC and refrigeration services
- post and browse equipment ads
- buy, sell, or request used/new/refurbished cooling equipment
- connect with businesses, installers, suppliers, dealers, and service providers
- use AI-powered tools to search, post, compare, and manage listings

The platform should become both:

1. a **trusted industry platform**
2. a **monetized marketplace business**

## Monetization Model

### Free Plans
- Buyer — Free
- Private Seller — Free

### Paid Plans
- Seller Plus — £9.99/month
- Trader Pro — £24.99/month
- Dealer / Business — £59.99/month

### Paid Boosts
- Bump Up
- Urgent
- Featured
- Top of Category
- Homepage Spotlight

### Revenue Strategy
Primary monetization at launch:

- subscriptions
- premium visibility
- listing boosts
- lead generation

Commission-based payments can be considered later, but **not required for MVP launch**.

## AI-First Direction
The platform should be built with AI deeply integrated into the product.

### Buyer AI
- natural-language marketplace search
- service matching
- recommendation and comparison help
- FAQ/help assistant

### Seller AI
- listing title generation
- description writing
- category suggestions
- condition/spec suggestions
- photo/content assistance

### Business AI
- profile writing
- lead reply assistance
- FAQ generation
- review response assistance

### Admin AI
- spam detection
- moderation assistance
- duplicate/risk detection
- support triage
- SEO/content gap analysis

### AI Principle
Use AI where it adds real value.
Do **not** rely on thin AI pages or low-quality auto-generated content.

---

## SEO Direction
The platform should be **SEO-led**, but not spam-led.

Core SEO assets:

- category pages
- subcategory pages
- business profile pages
- listing pages
- service pages
- wanted ad pages
- guide pages
- FAQ/help content
- location/category combinations where useful

The site should focus on:

- real pages
- real listings
- real businesses
- useful content
- strong internal linking
- structured data where appropriate
